Design: FFT-256 Accelerator
Target throughput: 1 sample per 10 clock cycles
Clock: 100 MHz (10 ns period)
Memory: Dual-port BRAM, 256 entries x 32-bit
Interface: AXI-Stream, 32-bit data width
Compute: 8-stage butterfly, radix-2
Critical path: Butterfly multiply-accumulate

Constraints from system integration:
  - Total latency must not exceed 10 us (10000 ns)
  - Area budget: 50000 LUTs (shared die with other IPs)
  - Power budget: 500 mW (thermal envelope)

Notes:
  - Unroll > 8 saturates BRAM read ports on this architecture
  - Pipeline depth > 4 gives diminishing returns due to butterfly dependency
  - Array partitioning beyond 4 increases routing congestion significantly
  - Clock period below 5 ns may not meet timing closure on target FPGA
